You would be forgiven for thinking this is a complicated answer. Let's look at each numbe and break it down.
the denominator is \(52!\) because that is the number of ways a deck of 52 cards can be shuffled.
the 12 comes from the total number of cards that cannot appear before our first ace.
4 is the number of Aces
